Who we are

We are the students of Management of Technology class at the University of AKron.

Catch the discussion and debates by top speakers on the inclusion of Arts in STEM, Role of Arts in STEM, Women in STEM and a lot of other intresting and intriguing topics. Welcome to this technical symposiyum where we talk about the ways to address the growing need of STEM education in US.
