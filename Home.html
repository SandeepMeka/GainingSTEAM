This Year's Theme "Re-innovating STEM through ART"

STEM is important for innovation. But STEM alone can not with stand the rapid changes in technology and the competion from others. To improve the innovation we need an artistic approach. Adding Arts to STEM increases the innovation as creativity is combined with concepts. So we add ARTS to STEM and make it STEAM. STEAM education can increase the innovative ability of an individual more than what STEM education can do. This is because the artistc side of a person can drive innovation greater than anything.
